About Northland ALX

Founded by student entrepreneur Elton John Kyeremeh and enterprise strategist Festus Amponsah Yeboah, Northland ALX represents a new paradigm in African technology innovation. We combine academic research with real-world business experience to create solutions that truly serve Africa's unique needs.

Our approach is distinctive: while our founder focuses on cutting-edge product design and development, our co-founder leverages deep enterprise relationships and market insights to ensure our innovations reach the organizations and communities that need them most. Together, we're building scalable, secure, and transformative technology solutions for governments, enterprises, and communities across Ghana and beyond.

Our Story

Northland ALX was born from a shared vision between two passionate innovators: Elton John Kyeremeh, a product designer and tech entrepreneur pursuing his IT studies, and Festus Amponsah Yeboah, a seasoned business professional with senior experience at Enterprise Insurance Ghana.

Our partnership combines cutting-edge technical innovation with deep market expertise. While Elton focuses on product design and development, creating solutions like GoldBod Systems for gold traceability and CTAP for educational access, Festus drives our operations, marketing strategy, and enterprise partnerships across Ghana's business landscape.

From our headquarters, we're building solutions that matter—IoT Compliance Modules for agricultural transparency, Email-to-Email Transfer Systems for financial inclusion, and North Warehousing for logistics optimization. We believe technology should serve Africa's unique needs, and every solution we create reflects this commitment to meaningful innovation.

As a student-founded company with enterprise backing, Northland ALX represents the new generation of African tech—where academic innovation meets business acumen to create lasting impact across the continent.
